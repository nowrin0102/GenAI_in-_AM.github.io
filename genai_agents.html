Exploring Foundation Model for AM

In order to explore foundation models for specific applications in AM, researchers should follow these steps:

Identifying the AM Task

Defining the scope of the problem, challenges, and establishing benchmarking metrics. The choice of model depends on the task, e.g., for image classification, GPT-4o; for image generation, DALL·E.

Benchmarking Metrics (Qualitative) Benchmarking Metrics (Quantitative)

Selecting a Base Model

Comparison of different AI models, including LLMs, GenAI models, diffusion models, GPT-4o, OpenAI O1, and DALL·E. Selecting the AI models best suited for the task.

Benchmarking Tools

Assessing the Base Model

Evaluating models using initial questions and zero-shot prompting.

Prompt Engineering

Exploring and refining prompts to optimize model responses, including Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
