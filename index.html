Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) in Additive Manufacturing (AM)

Goal

Exploration of GenAI and foundation models in AM. This includes building foundation models for AM and addressing key challenges such as design optimization, process monitoring, and defect detection.

Overview

Generative AI (GenAI) and foundation models are a rapidly evolving field. GenAI gets attention for its potential in various domains including Additive Manufacturing (AM). This website serves as a resource for understanding how GenAI and foundation models can be leveraged to improve AM processes. It focuses on:

    1.Building and exploring foundation models for AM

  • 2.Developing GenAI-powered solutions for AM
  • 3.Providing resources for adoption and implementation

Main Areas of Focus

1. Building and Exploring Foundation Models for AM

This section outlines the process of developing foundation models specifically for AM. It consists of various projects, each demonstrating a unique application of GenAI in manufacturing. The aim is to showcase how AI can enhance efficiency, reliability, and intelligence in AM.

2. Exploring GenAI Agents in AM and Manufacturing

This section delves into the role of GenAI agents in AM and broader manufacturing processes. It features multiple projects that investigate the integration of AI-driven agents to optimize production workflows and decision-making.

3. Miscellaneous Projects

This section covers additional research areas, such as:
