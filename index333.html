Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) in Additive Manufacturing (AM)

Exploration of GenAI and foundation models and building foundation models in the AM domain.

Generative AI (GenAI) and foundation models are a rapidly evolving field. GenAI gets attention for its potential in various domains including Additive Manufacturing (AM). This website explores how GenAI models can be used in the field of AM. It also focuses on solving various challenges in AM, such as improving design, monitoring the printing process, and detecting defects. The website is organized into different projects, each focusing on a specific way GenAI can be used in AM. Through these projects, we aim to show how AI can make manufacturing smarter, more efficient, and more reliable.
